# Signal Priority for Replies

## By Growth Phase

| Phase | Primary Goal | Highest Signals | Best Reply Style |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cold start | Earn first distribution and profile curiosity | favorite, dwell, profile_click, follow_author | Sharp single-take replies on original posts in your lane |
| Growth | Escape follower-only reach | share trio, dwell, follow_author | Benchmark replies, how-to replies, strong counterpoints |
| Scale | Compound OON safely | share trio, dwell pair, quote, follow | Reference-quality replies, evidence-led disagreement |

## Signal Stack for Replies

The reply-specific signal stack (what we optimize for):

| Signal | Priority | How |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `favorite_score` | Highest | Make the reply likeable — insight, wit, or useful info |
| `profile_click_score` | High | Show rare expertise that makes them curious about you |
| `dwell_time` | High | Substance and specificity — not length for its own sake |
| `follow_author_score` | High | Imply you have a repeatable lane worth following |
| `reply_score` | Medium | End with something that invites response |
| `not_interested_score` | Critical (negative) | Stay on niche, never generic, never off-topic |
| `block_author_score` | Critical (negative) | Critique ideas not people, zero hostility |

## OON-Safe Controversy for Replies

Strongest safe angles:
- Benchmark beats myth
- Tradeoff beats slogan
- Architecture limit beats founder drama
- "Works until scale X, then breaks" beats personal callout

Avoid: humiliation, personal subtweets, ragebait, anything triggering mute/block/report.
