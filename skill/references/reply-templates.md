# Reply Templates and Lane Rules

## Reply Lane Rules

1. Prefer original posts. Thunder surfaces replies to originals most reliably.
2. Prefer fresh posts. Thunder inventory is recency-first.
3. Use replies to transfer trust, not to spray opinions. The best replies create profile clicks and follows from the right audience.
4. If replying inside a thread, prefer a branch directly under the root post.
5. Do not use plain retweets as a substitute for replies.

## Cold-Start Reply Targets

Target accounts that:
- Post original thoughts in your exact niche
- Get real replies, not just likes
- Are small enough that your reply is not buried instantly
- Have audiences you want in your embedding cluster

Sweet spot: niche operators, builders with active comment sections, maintainers and founders in your lane.

## Templates

### 1. Add Data
Use when the post is missing proof.
> We measured this on [real workload]. The surprise was [specific result]. The bottleneck was not [obvious thing] — it was [non-obvious thing].
Best for: profile clicks, follows, quoted replies.

### 2. Respectful Counterpoint
Use when you want debate without block risk.
> Best for [specific condition], agreed. But it breaks once you optimize for [constraint]. We hit that wall and solved it by [specific move].
Best for: replies, quotes, profile clicks.

### 3. Build On The Point
Use when you want association with the original idea.
> Building exactly this. The hard part has not been [obvious thing] — it has been [specific edge case]. We fixed it by [brief tactic].
Best for: follows, profile clicks, niche relevance.

### 4. Benchmark Reply
Use when numbers matter.
> We tested [A] vs [B]. Winner flips once you care about [latency, cost, accuracy]. Here is the weird part: [one surprising datapoint].
Best for: dwell, shares, quotes.

### 5. Tooling Recommendation
Use when someone is asking for options.
> If you optimize for [specific use case], pick [approach]. If you optimize for [other use case], pick [other approach]. Most people mix those up.
Best for: profile clicks, follows, reply quality.

## Reply vs Quote Decision

Reply when:
- The original post is fresh
- The original audience is your target audience
- Your take works best as an addition

Quote when:
- You have a standalone frame
- Your hook can travel without the original context
- You want quote_score and a new candidate
